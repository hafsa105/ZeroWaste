Steps: Turn it off Energy conservation is one of the most important things you can do to reduce your carbon footprint. Leaving your electricals on standby uses uneccessary energy – turn them off at the wall and you could see huge improvements. Switch to renewables by Choosing 100% renewable electricity is a great eco-friendly tip for any home – and is one of the biggest things you can do to help tackle the climate crisis. Unfortunately, due to the current situation in the energy market, it’s best to stay with your existing supplier right now. But you can sign up to our newsletter to stay up to date with news about renewables and climate action, and to hear when you’re able to switch again. A lot of mainstream cleaning products containing detergents, preservatives, or foaming agents are made from various toxic chemicals that wash up into streams and rivers, causing water pollution that enters ecosystems and damages biodiversity. Switching to products that contain sustainably grown or raised ingredients and non-synthetics reduces the risk of exposure to toxic chemicals that are harmful to humans as well as the environment. You can also easily make your own natural cleaners by mixing vinegar, lemon juice and bicarbonate of soda. This is a sustainable and cheaper alternative that reduces toxic chemicals and packaging waste in your home.
